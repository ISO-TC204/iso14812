Questions for METR Workshop 10: Multimodal and Micromobility Operations

The tenth METR workshop will include focus on the following questions:

  • What types of information does METR need to convey related to sidewalk, pedestrian plaza, and kerbside operations?
  • To what extent does METR need to convey additional regulated (allowed) uses of public transport facilities?
  • Who needs information on kerbside regulations and when?
  • What specific capabilities/information are needed within METR to accommodate travellers with disabilities?
  • What METR information needs to be conveyed to VRUs/VRVs about the allowed operation of AVs, especially footway delivery drones?
