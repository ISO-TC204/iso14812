What is the minimum requirement to be considered connected? <br/>1. Vehicle equipped with onboard unit<br/>2. The onboard unit is operational and physically connected (i.e., has not been tampered with)<br/>3. The onboard unit is on (e.g., has power and can broadcast/receive)<br/>4. The onboard equipment has at least one application process running<br/>5. The onboard unit has a basic vehicle safety application process running (i.e., perhaps receive only)<br/>6. It is transmitting its location per the basic safety application<br/><br/>Is a vehicle connected if it does not support local standards (i.e., transmitting BSMs in Europe)<br/>
